I spent the whole night sitting in the port and
watching the lights went down one by one inside the red brick walls,
while the campus lights remained until sunrise.
My mind roamed all over between inconsistent dots,
the girl reselling CDC products at back alleys,
the gunshot at the PASS gates of hiring site,
the next-door looking for cracked coding tutorials for her child,
the old man fishing with long rods on his balcony...
All of a sudden, my instinct asks me to move back to campus.
