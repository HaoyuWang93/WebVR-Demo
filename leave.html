I spent the whole night sitting in the port and
watching the lights went down one by one inside the red brick walls,
while the campus remained luminous until sunrise.
...
My mind roamed all over between inconsistent dots,
the girl reselling CDC rations at the back alley,
the gunshot at the PASS gates of hiring site,
the next-door looking for cracked coding tutorials for her kids,
the old man fishing with extra-long rods on his balcony...
...
All of a sudden, my instinct convinced me to move back to campus.
